About the Project

This project was to help us gain more practice using Adobe Premiere Pro, specifically, sound design. We were split into groups to share our favorite song then we were take one person's song and apply it to a clip of our choice with background sounds. I chose Meet Me in the Woods by Lord Huron and I immediately thought of a specific scene in the movie Wolf Children where a wolf-man revealed his true self in the woods to his love. I also chose this scene because there was a lot to work with for the background. I added wind, leaves, cloth, and even my own voice to the sound design. I adjusted the volumes to be in timing with sudden wind gusts, cloth movement, and character movement. Though I believe it's not my best work because it was a quick assignment we had to do, I enjoyed it a lot and I beleive I can do better with sound design today.
