About the Project

CLICK HERE to view the video. This is one of my first times using Blender. This was a short project we did in Art 74 and the goal was to experiment with making simple shape models and adding key frames to rotate or move the shapes from point A to B. I wanted to include this video, even though it is extremely basic, to show that this is the very beginning of my 3D modeling experience. Furthermore, I did this by myself with only a demo from the professor. This assignment helped me grasp the basics of key framing and making videos from our Blender projects. This led to key framing the other Blender Projects by myself.
